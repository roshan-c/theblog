TMZ: GART FOUND DEAD AT 20

october 20th 2024

gart stone, the once respected developer and community manager of the gartpack (previously known as garypack) expansion for lethal company was found at his desk with a severe bullet wound in his head

many speculate that gart took his own life after the immense pressure for his latest release (gartpack : the master scrap collection) collapsed on himself after launch. critics cited it's poor performance, and a number of 'red-flags' in it's content, as reasons that it flopped

gart's last words were "i just wanted to overdeliver", and it is clear that he was trying his best. our thoughts are with his family and friends at this time
